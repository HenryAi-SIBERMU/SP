#### Metodologi yang Perlu Dielaborasi:

**A. Inventarisasi & Pemetaan Area**

1. **Data Sources**:
   - Google Earth Pro / Google Earth Engine (free, resolusi tinggi)
   - OpenStreetMap (OSM) untuk data infrastruktur transportasi
   - Government datasets: Peta Jaringan TransJakarta, Stasiun KRL (KAI), MRT/LRT
   - Satelit imagery: Sentinel-2 (10m resolution, free) atau Planet Labs (3m, paid)

2. **GIS Tools**:
   - QGIS (open-source) atau ArcGIS (licensed)
   - Python libraries: geopandas, rasterio, shapely

3. **Sampling Strategy**:
   - **Total enumeration**: Untuk infrastruktur terbatas (halte, stasiun) → inventory semua
   - **Stratified sampling**: Untuk parkiran terbuka dan MSCP → sample per kategori (mall, perkantoran, kampus, rumah sakit)

4. **Area Measurement**:
   - Digitize polygon di GIS
   - Calculate gross area vs net usable area
   - Usable area ratio: 70-85% (akun untuk shading, spacing, maintenance access)

**B. Konversi Area ke Kapasitas**

**Formula Standar**:
```
Kapasitas (kWp) = Usable Area (m²) × Module Efficiency × Irradiance / 1000

Atau simplified:
Kapasitas (kWp) = Usable Area (m²) × 0.15 hingga 0.20 kWp/m²
```

**Asumsi untuk Indonesia**:
- Module efficiency: 18-22% (mono-crystalline silicon, current tech)
- Module power: 400-550 Wp per panel
- Panel size: ~2 m² per panel
- Installation density: 0.18 kWp/m² (conservative) atau 0.20 kWp/m² (optimistic)

**C. Estimasi Produksi Energi**

**Formula**:
```
Produksi Tahunan (kWh) = Kapasitas (kWp) × Peak Sun Hours × 365 × Performance Ratio
```

**Parameter untuk Jabodetabek**:
- **Peak Sun Hours (PSH)**: 4.5 - 5.0 kWh/m²/day (data dari PVGIS atau NASA POWER)
- **Performance Ratio (PR)**: 75-80% (account untuk losses: inverter, cable, soiling, temperature)
- **Annual degradation**: 0.5-0.7% per tahun (untuk analisis lifetime)

**Tools untuk Estimasi**:
- **PVGIS** (EU Joint Research Centre): Free, data global, Indonesia coverage ✅
- **PVWatts** (NREL, USA): Free, tapi US-centric
- **SAM** (System Advisor Model, NREL): Advanced, free
- **HelioScope** atau **PVsyst**: Professional, paid

**Rekomendasi**: Gunakan PVGIS untuk quick estimation, validasi dengan data aktual dari existing installation di Indonesia

**D. Validasi & Uncertainty**

- Compare dengan actual data dari Bandara Soetta, stasiun KRL
- Sensitivity analysis: bagaimana jika PR berbeda ±5%, PSH berbeda ±10%
- Monte Carlo simulation untuk uncertainty quantification (optional, advanced)

### 8. Tantangan (Halaman 8) ⚠️ STATUS: TOO BRIEF

#### 1. Tantangan Teknis

**A. Maintenance & Operations**
- **Cleaning requirement**: Jakarta dusty + pollution → panel perlu cleaning 3-4x/tahun untuk maintain efficiency
- **Access for maintenance**: 
  - Halte & stasiun: bagaimana access safely tanpa ganggu operasional?
  - Parking canopy: perlu lift/scaffold untuk cleaning

- **Spare parts availability**: Inverter, combiner box, etc. → local supply chain

**B. Structural Issues**
- **Wind load calculation**: Jakarta occasionally strong winds → need proper structural engineering
- **Seismic design**: Indonesia zona gempa → mounting system harus comply dengan SNI
- **Retrofit constraint**: Many existing JPO & halte struktur lama → perlu structural assessment & possible reinforcement
- **Weight consideration**: Solar panel + mounting + snow/water accumulation (walaupun Jakarta jarang hujan deras lama)

**C. Electrical Integration**
- **Grid connection point**: Dimana connect ke grid PLN? Nearby transformer capacity?
- **Voltage compatibility**: Three-phase vs single-phase
- **Inverter sizing & placement**: Inverter harus protected dari weather, perlu space
- **Cable routing**: Underground conduit atau overhead? Coordination dengan existing utilities

**D. Vandalism & Security**
- Public infrastructure vulnerable to theft (copper cable, panel)
- Need security design: panel height, anti-theft mounting, CCTV

**E. Shading & Orientation**
- Not all locations optimal: some shaded by buildings, trees
- Orientation bisa east-west di beberapa infrastruktur (JPO crossing north-south road)

---

#### 2. Pembiayaan

**A. High CAPEX**
- **Solar carport CAPEX**: Rp 18-22 juta/kWp (vs rooftop Rp 12-15 juta/kWp)
- **Why more expensive?**: 
  - Carport structure: steel/aluminum frame, foundation, canopy design
  - Engineering complexity: wind load, spacing for vehicle clearance
- **Total investment**: 857 MWp × Rp 18 juta = **Rp 15.4 triliun** (~USD 1 billion)

**B. Sumber Pembiayaan - Options**

**1. Public Budget (APBD/APBN)**
- Untuk infrastruktur publik (halte, stasiun, JPO, pedestrian)
- Challenge: competing priorities, limited fiscal space
- Advantage: No need profit motive, long-term public benefit

**2. KPBU (Kerjasama Pemerintah dan Badan Usaha) / PPP**
- Risk sharing antara government & private sector
- Government: land/infrastructure, regulatory support, offtake guarantee
- Private: financing, construction, operation
- Revenue: dari energy sales atau availability payment

**3. BOOT (Build-Own-Operate-Transfer)**
- Private developer invest & operate 15-20 tahun
- Selling electricity ke infrastructure owner atau ke grid
- Transfer ownership to government setelah concession period
- Mitigasi risk: PPA (Power Purchase Agreement) jangka panjang

**4. Green Bonds / Sukuk Hijau**
- Instrument financing untuk green projects
- Interest rate bisa lebih rendah (investor willing accept lower return untuk ESG)
- Indonesia sudah issue sovereign green bond, could be extended untuk municipal/corporate level

**5. Corporate Self-Investment (untuk parking lot komersial)**
- Mall, perkantoran invest sendiri
- Payback period: 6-8 tahun (tergantung electricity tariff & solar productivity)
- ROI attractive jika self-consumption tinggi

**6. Crowdfunding / Community Solar**
- Public contribute small investment untuk specific project
- Return: share of energy savings atau carbon credit
- Good untuk awareness & public engagement

**7. International Climate Finance**
- Green Climate Fund (GCF), Asian Development Bank (ADB), World Bank
- Grant atau concessional loan untuk developing countries
- Requirement: strong project proposal, co-financing

**C. Business Model Innovation**

**Integration dengan EV Charging**
- Solar carport + EV charger → dual revenue stream
- Tarif charging bisa premium (orang willing bayar untuk convenience)
- Future-proof: anticipate EV adoption growth

**Carbon Credit Monetization**
- Indonesia carbon market (IDXCarbon) mulai operasional
- Setiap MWh solar → carbon credit → additional revenue
- Bisa dijual ke corporate yang need offset emission

#### 3. Regulasi

**A. Interkoneksi PLN**
- **PERMEN ESDM 26/2021**: Regulasi PLTS Atap
  - Net-metering ratio: 65% (ekspor ke grid hanya dibayar 65% dari tarif)
  - Kapasitas max: 100% dari daya tersambang
  - Prosedur aplikasi interconnection bisa lama (3-6 bulan)
  
- **Challenge**:
  - Bureaucracy process masih panjang
  - PLN sometimes reluctant untuk approve large-scale rooftop (concern tentang grid stability)
  - Export limitation: jika produksi > consumption, excess energy "lost"

- **Rekomendasi**:
  - Streamline interconnection process untuk public infrastructure
  - Better export compensation (65% → 85-90%?)

**B. Ownership & Right-of-Way**
- **Infrastruktur publik**: 
  - Halte, stasiun → ownership clear (TransJ, KAI, MRT)

  - Perlu legal framework: apakah swasta bisa operate solar di public asset?

- **Private land**:
  - Parking lot, MSCP → clear, no issue

- **Mixed ownership** (pedestrian canopy):
  - Coordinating multiple stakeholders challenging
  - Need regulatory clarity tentang right-of-way di pedestrian area

**C. Building Code & Safety Standards**
- **Structural permit**: 
  - Retrofit solar di existing building perlu IMB tambahan?
  - Structural engineering approval dari Dinas PU
  - Seismic compliance (SNI gempa)

  - Sertifikasi electrical installation (SLO - Sertifikat Laik Operasi)

  - Fire safety consideration

- **Standards harmonization**:
  - IEC standards untuk solar component vs SNI
  - Need clear guidance untuk installer & developer

**Rekomendasi Policy**:
